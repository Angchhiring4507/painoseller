Vintage Pianos

Vintage Pianos, founded in 1971, stands as a venerable institution with a legacy spanning over five decades. Since its inception, the company has been at the forefront of the piano industry, renowned for its unwavering commitment to providing quality instruments that resonate with timeless elegance.
As Vintage Pianos continues to embark on its journey, the commitment to excellence remains unwavering. The company looks forward to sharing the joy of music with generations to come, ensuring that the timeless allure of vintage pianos continues to echo through the corridors of musical history.

In 1971, Vintage Pianos was born from a shared passion for music and a dream to craft instruments that would stand the test of time. A group of visionaries, driven by the enchantment of melodies, came together to create pianos that would not only fill spaces with beautiful music but also tell stories of artistry and dedication.
From the very first piano crafted, our commitment to excellence resonated in every key. Vintage Pianos quickly became a symbol of tradition and innovation, with each instrument being a masterpiece that encapsulates the spirit of its time.
